Pink Bubble Generator

Description:

This is a generative web application that creates circles, or in this case bubbles, to make a design. You are able to control the size of the circles, the colors, and the opacity.

Parameters:

  • circleDiameter: Change the size of the circle.
  • colorRed: Change how much red is in the circle color.
  • colorGreen: Change how much green is in the circle color.
  • colorBlue: Change how much blue is in the circle color.
  • opacity: Change how transparent the circle is.
